I would just like to inform all of you who take the time to read our blogs, about a few features most people don't know about our free office software.

To start of with, as most of you know every good piece of software needs a great print preview. Well here it is, our software doesn't just have a normal print preview, they have the best print previews in the productivity software industry, and here is why...

Our print previews have a green printing option which will save you tons of money, on ink and time, when printing your documents and spreadsheets. Below you will see the option shown on our print previews. The options for printing are for a single page, two pages, or four pages of your digital document to be printed on one physical paper page. Now that is very nice and handy!



Our second less known feature of our software is that our four main applications in each office suite are capable of transparency. With our Excalibur office suite the transparency is set from the main desktop dock. Just right-click and select your transparency option from the short-cut menu.


With our other office suites and individual software applications WordGraph, Penumbra, and Accel spreadsheet, you need to go to the installation folder and find the "DesktopDock.ini" file. Open this file in NotePad by right-clicking on the ini file and select the "Open with..." option.


You will see the following option inside the ini file:


Set your transparency options in 10, 20, 30 etc. upto 90. Any higher than 90 and the whole application will become completely invisible.
