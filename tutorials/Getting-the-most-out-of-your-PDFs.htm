Getting the most out of your PDFs

SSuite Apps

It seems that the PDF document format is going to be one of the new standards in document exchange, whether online or on your local desktop environment, so with that in mind I went out and searched the internet for the perfect code base to create/export PFD documents straight from our best word processors.


We have recently updated our office suites and stand-alone word processors to not just export your documents as Image based PDF documents(Standard Quality), but they are now capable of exporting your documents as pure high quality PDF(High Quality) documents.



Most of the current popular web browsers, e.g. Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, Vivaldi etc. , are now able to open PDF documents without the need for an Adobe Reader application or specialized software to be installed on your mobile device or desktop computer. The PDF format is going to be one of the most used and exhangeable document formats next to the HTML5 document format for the forseeable future.


With all of the above in mind, you are now ready to create/export any of your documents to PDF, using any of our professional word processors or office suites and be confident whom ever opens your document will view and experience it just as you intended. There will be no more formatting errors or software downloads necessary to open PDF documents, no matter what device your are using.
