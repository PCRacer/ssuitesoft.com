Advertising from major companies such as Google and other prominent marketing firms often disrupts website layouts and consumes significant amounts of data bandwidth. Additionally, these ads frequently track user's browsing activities, compromising privacy. It is crucial to take measures to mitigate these issues.

Fortunately, users can effectively address these problems with a single application add-on or plug-in designed to block unwanted ads. This solution is not only efficient but also available at no cost, providing a straightforward means to enhance both the browsing experience and data privacy.


As you may already be aware, upon installing one of our browsers, such as NetSurfer Extreme or NetSurfer E-Gamer, you can effortlessly access the latest versions of adblocker extensions by visiting our Extension Library. These updates are available for seamless installation. We support both the v2 and v3 manifest editions of uBlock, which in our opinion is currently the best and most secure way of surfing the internet!


Using adblockers in a browser offers several notable advantages:

  • Improved Browsing Experience: Adblockers remove unwanted ads, leading to a cleaner, less cluttered interface and a more pleasant browsing experience.
  • Faster Page Load Times: By blocking ads, which can be heavy and numerous, adblockers help speed up page load times, making browsing more efficient.
  • Enhanced Privacy: Many ads and tracking scripts collect personal data for targeted advertising. Adblockers reduce the risk of data collection and enhance your online privacy.
  • Reduced Distractions: Blocking ads minimizes interruptions and distractions, allowing users to focus on content without being sidetracked by intrusive pop-ups and banners.
  • Less Risk of Malware: Ads can sometimes contain malware or lead to malicious sites. Adblockers can help protect your device by blocking such harmful content.

Overall, adblockers streamline and secure your internet experience, making it faster and more private.

Blocking advertisements in Chromium-based browsers like Google Chrome, MS Edge, and Vivaldi can be efficiently done using browser extensions like uBlock Origin.
